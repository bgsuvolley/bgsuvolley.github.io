BGSU Traditions

The Fight Song

Ay Ziggy Zoomba

Roll Along, You BG Warriors
Roll Along, and Win for BGSU!

Its history is tied to a WWII veteran and musicologist, Gilbert Fox, who, while serving in Italy, learned a Zulu war chant from South African airmen. In 1946, he loosely translated and adapted this chant into "Ay Ziggy Zoomba." The song has since become a beloved BGSU tradition, with the BGSU Undergraduate Student Government formally supporting its official status as a fight song in 2013.
